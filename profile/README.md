# Kreager Digital Transformation Industry‑X: Open Source Program

Welcome to the **Kreager Digital Transformation Industry‑X (KDTIX)** open source program! This community‑led initiative builds and maintains open‑source projects that accelerate digital transformation, engineering productivity and AI innovation across industries.

## About us

KDTIX is a collective of engineers, researchers and contributors dedicated to producing high‑quality, well‑documented software. Our projects span domains from cloud‑native development and DevOps automation to machine‑learning tooling and modular engineering guides.

Our mission is to:

* Create reliable, maintainable open‑source software under permissive licenses.
* Foster a welcoming, inclusive community for contributors of all backgrounds and experience levels.
* Encourage collaboration, transparency and innovation.
* Support the broader open‑source ecosystem through contributions and mentorship.

## Getting involved

Contributions of all kinds are welcome—new features, bug fixes, documentation improvements and more. Please review our [contributing guidelines](../CONTRIBUTING.md) for details on how to get started, and take a look at our [code of conduct](../CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) to understand the expectations we have for everyone in the community.

If you’re new to open source, check out issues labelled `good first issue` across our repositories. These tasks are designed to help new contributors get started quickly. We also encourage you to join discussions in our issues and pull requests, suggest improvements and share your feedback.

## Stay connected

* **Email** – questions and suggestions: [Open.Source@kdtix.com](mailto:Open.Source@kdtix.com)
* **Sponsorship** – if you or your organisation benefit from our projects, please consider [supporting our work](../FUNDING.yml). Your contributions help sustain and grow KDTIX’s open‑source initiatives.

Thank you for your interest in KDTIX and for being part of our open‑source journey!
