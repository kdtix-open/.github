# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We, as members, contributors and leaders of KDTIX open‑source projects,
pledge to make participation in our community a harassment‑free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible
disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and
expression, level of experience, education, socio‑economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion or sexual identity and
orientation【375064118867009†L0-L12】.  We pledge to act and interact in ways
that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive and healthy
community【375064118867009†L11-L26】.

## Our Standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment for
our community include:

* Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people【375064118867009†L16-L20】
* Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints and experiences【375064118867009†L20-L21】
* Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback【375064118867009†L21-L22】
* Accepting responsibility and apologising to those affected by our mistakes,
  and learning from the experience【375064118867009†L22-L25】
* Focusing on what is best not just for us as individuals but for the
  overall community【375064118867009†L24-L25】

Examples of unacceptable behaviour include:

* The use of sexualised language or imagery and sexual attention or
  advances of any kind【375064118867009†L27-L31】
* Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments and personal or political attacks【375064118867009†L31-L33】
* Public or private harassment【375064118867009†L31-L33】
* Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or email
  address, without their explicit permission【375064118867009†L33-L35】
* Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting【375064118867009†L35-L36】

## Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may
be reported to the maintainers responsible for enforcement at
[Open.Source@kdtix.com](mailto:Open.Source@kdtix.com).  All complaints will be
reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.  Maintainers are
obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter of any
incident【375064118867009†L60-L66】.  Additional escalation guidelines
are described in our enforcement section.

## Enforcement Guidelines

Community leaders will follow these Community Impact Guidelines in
determining the consequences for any action they deem in violation of
this Code of Conduct.  Consequences may range from a private
correction to a permanent ban from all KDTIX community spaces, as
detailed in the [Contributor Covenant 2.0](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/0/code_of_conduct.html).
